Instructions to play Simon:
1. Objective: Repeat the sequence of lights and sounds generated by the computer.

2. How to play:
  • Press "Play" to start.
  • Observe and listen to the sequence.
  • Repeat the sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order.

3. Buttons:
  • The colored buttons represent the sequence.
  • There are four colors: red, green, blue, and yellow.

4. Winning:
  • Continue repeating the sequence correctly.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends.

That's it! Pay attention, remember the sequence, and challenge your memory.
Good luck!
